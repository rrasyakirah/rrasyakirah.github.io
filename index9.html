How to make Beef Yakiniku with Broccoli

Easy, Economical, and Practical

Let's cook at home using simple ingredients with the taste is no less delicious than in a restaurant.

Learn more

You Will Need This

Ingredients

  • 250gram sliced beef
  • 1 stalk of broccoli
  • 1 cloves of onion, cut lengtwise
  • 1 green paprika, remove the seed, cut lengtwise
  • 1 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp of sweet soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp of pepper powder
  • 1 tbsp of honey
  • 2 tbsp of sesame oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, finely choppe it
  • 1 cm of ginger, finely choppe it
  • 200ml of meat stew
  • salt and sugar to taste
  • 3 tbsp of oil for frying

Tools

  • Frying pan
  • Pan
  • Stove
  • Fork
  • Spoon
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Cobek
  • Bowl

Step

  • Boil the meat until tender
  • Strain the beef stew until the volume is 200ml, then set aside
  • Cut the garlic, then combine with ginger and crush until smooth
  • Add 3 tbsp of cooking oil to the frying pan
  • Saute garlic and ginger until fragrant and stir until evenly
  • And add meat, mix well, add 200ml meat stew
  • Cut 1 onion lengthwise
  • Add 1 green paprika cut lengthwise and remove seed
  • Add oyster sauce(FYI : 1 sachet of oyster sauce same than 1 tbsp)
  • Add 1 tbsp of sweet soy sauce
  • Add 1 tbsp of honey and mix everything well
  • Add 2 tbsp of sesame oil and mix everything well
  • Add 1/2 tsp of pepper powder
  • Add 1 stalk of broccoli that has been cut into pieces and mix everything well
  • Add 1 clove of onion and 1 choppe green paprika and mix everything well
  • Add salt just enough
  • And then add sugar just enough, mix everything well
  • Cook for a few minutes to allow the flavors to infuse
  • Last step is serve with warm white rice
